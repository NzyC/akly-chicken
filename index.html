About Us

At Akly Charcoal Chicken, we are passionate about bringing the authentic flavours of Lebanon to our customers in Riverwood. Our family-owned restaurant has been a fixture in the community for years, and we pride ourselves on offering high-quality, delicious food and a welcoming atmosphere.
Our menu features a variety of traditional dishes, including savory charcoal chicken, succulent shawarma, and a selection of dips and spreads that are perfect for sharing. Everything we serve is made with the freshest ingredients and cooked using traditional techniques, ensuring that every bite is bursting with flavour.
